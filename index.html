Software Engineer

I am an Aerospace Engineer turned Software Engineer. I have a Master’s degree in Aeronautics and Astronautics from Stanford University. I worked on my career transition mostly through self-learning and with the help of Hackbright Academy’s all-women coding bootcamp. I previously worked in the Player Developer Experience team at Electronic Arts on backend engineering tools and platforms that game developers use. I primarily code in Python.

Of course, I'm a feminist. I live in the San Francisco bay area with my handsome husband, Soumyaroop, and our dog, Turing, named after Alan Turing. I love solving 3D puzzles and playing with Turing.
